Rules of Thumb Practical guardrails
  • Set the loss limit before you play, then stop at that number.
  • Never chase overdue lottery numbers or casino losses.
  • Prefer games where strategy lowers the house edge: blackjack, video poker, craps line bets with odds.
  • Skip side bets unless you are intentionally paying for the thrill.
  • Keep a note of bought lines so you do not accidentally duplicate a ticket.
